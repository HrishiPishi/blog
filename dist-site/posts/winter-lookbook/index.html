Winter Lookbook — Layers

soft

what i wore this winter. mostly grey, mostly wool, one loud mistake.

The pieces

  • Coat — boiled-wool chore coat, oversized, the anchor of everything
  • Knit — fisherman's gansey, ecru, worn until it earned its holes
  • Trouser — single-pleat flannel, cuffed high
  • Boot — service boots, resoled twice, never polished
  • Loud mistake — a scarf the color of a traffic cone. No notes. Kept it.

Notes on the board

Each tile below is a separate look, shot on the same grey wall so the clothes do the talking. The point of a uniform isn't sameness — it's removing one decision so you can spend it elsewhere. Five pieces, a winter's worth of mornings, almost no thinking required.

The gallery layout treats every image as an indexed plate, the way a contact sheet or a print proof would. Untitled, dated, numbered.
