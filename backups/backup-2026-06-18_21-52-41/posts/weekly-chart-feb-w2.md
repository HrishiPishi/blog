What was actually on repeat this week. No scores, no reviews — just the rotation,
ranked by how often I reached for it.

## Heavy rotation

1. **Tirzah** — *Devotion* — still the warmest lo-fi on the shelf
2. **Duster** — *Stratosphere* — for the grey afternoons
3. **Alice Coltrane** — *Journey in Satchidananda* — Monday mornings, every time
4. **Yves Tumor** — *Heaven to a Tortured Mind* — the only thing loud enough
5. **Grouper** — *Ruins* — last thing before sleep

## On the way out

- **Cocteau Twins** — *Heaven or Las Vegas* — overplayed, needs a rest
- **DJ Sabrina the Teenage DJ** — *Charmed* — great, but four hours is a lot

## Single of the week

**"Saturn"** — a slow build that never quite resolves, which is the point. Looped
it maybe thirty times. Filing it next to the things I keep coming back to without
being able to explain why.
